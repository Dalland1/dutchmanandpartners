Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services, processes, and how we can assist you in sports law matters.

What types of clients do you represent?
We represent a diverse range of clients in the sports industry, including professional athletes, sports clubs, federations, agents, marketing agencies, and sports organizations. Our expertise covers both individual and corporate legal needs.
How do you handle international sports law cases?
Our team has extensive experience with international sports law, including working with governing bodies like FIFA, UEFA, IOC, and WADA, and representing clients before the Court of Arbitration for Sport (CAS). We have a global network to assist with cross-border legal challenges.
What is your fee structure?
Our fee structure is transparent and can be tailored to the specific needs of each client and case. We offer various arrangements, including hourly rates, fixed fees for specific services, and success-based fees where appropriate. We'll discuss this in detail during your initial consultation.
How quickly can I get a consultation?
We prioritize urgent matters and aim to schedule initial consultations as quickly as possible, usually within 24-48 hours. Please contact us via our form or phone, and our team will get back to you promptly.
